Altered Items (Control Video Game)

Altered Items are ordinary objects which have been acted upon by paranatural forces during Altered World Events, resulting in the development of unique and sometimes dangerous properties. They are distinguished from Objects of Power in that they lack an inherent connection to the Board, or the ability for parautilitarians to bind to them and wield their abilities. Altered Items were studied and contained by the Federal Bureau of Control in the Panopticon, in the Sector, as well as in other areas of the Oldest House. The term "Altered Item" is modern FBC terminology. Prior to the death of Director Theodore Ash, Sr. in 1964, the FBC employed the term "Possessed Object" instead, which reflected the organization's origins in occultism.
