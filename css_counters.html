Objects of Power (From Control the Video Game)

Objects of Power (OoPs) are a type of Altered Item connected to the Astral Plane, although Emily Pope believes they may be connected to other paranatural forces. Unlike ordinary Altered Items, many OoPs can be bound by parautilitarians, allowing them to wield their abilities. Objects of Power are of great importance to the Federal Bureau of Control, and many are contained within the Oldest House.
